Poulsbo, WA, USA

Location

To get to Poulsbo, take the ferry from Seattle to Bainbridge Island and drive about 12 miles.

About

Weather

During the spring and summer, it is so nice and bright outside. During winter and fall, the air gets chilly but makes up for beautiful scenerey.

Food

One of the best places to eat is Sluys Bakery, the business has been running since the 1900's and it is a popular choice for tourists. One of best local bakeries around.

Sports

We all love the Seahawks but our local love comes to the high school with the North Kitsap Vikings football and basketball team.
